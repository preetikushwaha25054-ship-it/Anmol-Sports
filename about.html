Know More About Anmol Sports

Our Story

Anmol Sports was founded in **20/08/2023** with a simple goal: to provide high-quality, reliable sports equipment to athletes of all levels. What started as a small local shop selling basic gear has grown into a trusted national supplier, driven by our passion for sports and commitment to performance. We believe that the right equipment can make all the difference, helping you push boundaries and achieve greatness.

**Our Roots:** With over 3, 4 years of experience, we opened our first shop in Aadarsha Nagar, Kawardha. Since then, we have been supporting not just the gear, but also the dreams of players. We consider our customers family and are committed to providing the best sporting experience.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to empower the next generation of athletes by making premium sports gear accessible and affordable. We envision a future where everyone has the tools they need to pursue their passion for fitness and sport.

Quality Commitment

We source products only from certified and reputable manufacturers worldwide.

Customer Focus

We provide personalized advice and dedicated support to meet every customer's unique needs.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, honesty, and fairness in all our dealings.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of sports enthusiasts and experts, led by **Mr. Jitendra Singh Netam**. Our knowledge spans across various sports, ensuring that we offer you the best recommendations and the latest innovations in sports technology.

We are not just sellers; we are players, coaches, and fans, deeply invested in the success of the sports community.

Meet the Man Behind Anmol Sports

Mr. Jitendra Singh Netam

Mr. Jitendra Singh Netam is the driving force and heart of Anmol Sports. With a personal history as a passionate cricketer, he opened the shop in 20/08/2023 to share his love for sports and commitment to quality gear with the local community.

He personally ensures every piece of equipment meets the highest standards. When you visit the shop, you'll likely find him there, offering expert advice and a warm welcome!
